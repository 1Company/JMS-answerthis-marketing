## FAQ

### Is AnswerThis really free?
Yes. Free tier includes up to 50 participants per session, unlimited sessions. No credit card required.

### What if I need polls occasionally?
AnswerThis is for Q&A only. If you regularly need polls, Slido might be better for you. If polls are rare, use a free tool like Mentimeter alongside AnswerThis.

### How does the speaker display work?
Our speaker display is designed for visibility from stage. Large text, high contrast, one-click question selection. No squinting at tiny laptop text.

### Can I import questions from Slido?
Not currently, but Q&A sessions start fresh anyway. Your attendees won't notice any difference.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption. Questions are deleted after 30 days unless you export them.
