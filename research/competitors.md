# AnswerThis Competitor Analysis

*Last updated: 2026-01-28*

## Market Overview

The live Q&A and audience engagement market is dominated by a few major players, but there's room for focused, simpler alternatives.

## Direct Competitors

### 1. Slido (sli.do) - MAJOR
- **Owner:** Cisco (acquired 2020)
- **Features:** Q&A, polls, quizzes, word clouds, surveys
- **Integrations:** Zoom, Teams, Webex, Google Slides
- **Pricing:** Free (limited) → $12.50/mo → Enterprise
- **Strength:** Brand recognition, integrations
- **Weakness:** Can be overwhelming, Cisco enterprise focus

### 2. Mentimeter
- **Features:** Interactive presentations, Q&A, polls, word clouds
- **Focus:** Presentation-first (not just Q&A)
- **Pricing:** Free → $11.99/mo → Teams plans
- **Strength:** Beautiful UI, presentation features
- **Weakness:** Less focused on pure Q&A

### 3. Pigeonhole Live
- **Features:** Q&A, polls, surveys, chat
- **Focus:** Town halls, conferences
- **Pricing:** Event-based pricing
- **Strength:** Enterprise features, analytics

### 4. Wooclap
- **Features:** Interactive Q&A, polls, quizzes
- **Focus:** Education sector
- **Pricing:** Free tier → Paid plans

### 5. Poll Everywhere
- **Features:** Live polls, Q&A, surveys
- **Integrations:** PowerPoint, Keynote, Google Slides
- **Pricing:** Free → Paid plans

### 6. Vevox
- **Features:** Q&A, polling, surveys
- **Focus:** Education + Corporate
- **Integrations:** MS Teams, LMS platforms

## AnswerThis Differentiators

### Current Advantages
- ✅ **Simplicity** - Focused on Q&A only, not cluttered
- ✅ **No registration** for attendees (just name/email)
- ✅ **Speaker display** - Purpose-built for readability
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** for moderators
- ✅ **Free/affordable** - No enterprise bloat

### Positioning Opportunity
> "Slido is great, but it's too much. AnswerThis is Q&A done right."

## Target Market Analysis

### Sweet Spot
1. **Small-medium seminars** (50-500 attendees)
2. **Independent speakers/trainers**
3. **Companies without Cisco/MS ecosystem**
4. **One-time events** (conferences, workshops)

### Avoid (for now)
- Enterprise (Slido owns this)
- Education (Wooclap, Mentimeter strong)
